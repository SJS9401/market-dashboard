→ **CapEx 폭증 전환점 분기**: TTM CapEx **$147.3B (+67% YoY)**, Q1 26 단독 **$44.2B** (PP&E gross), cash CapEx $43.2B. **TTM FCF $1.2B (-95% YoY)** = $25.9B → $1.2B 거의 zero.
